Think Global

Think Global's Purpose

The purpose of Think Global is to raise cultural awareness about differences between people's beliefs and religion through educational and cultural exchange programmes for students. These programmes are designed to present the opportunity to understand and learn various societal and cultural aspects of different nations. Through the programmes, attendants can develop themselves through cross-cultural experiences abroad while working towards education or a cause important to attendants for personal and professional development.

The core values of Think Global are acceptance, respect and appreciation of the cultural diversity that exists around the world. Think Global's vision is a world where everyone is accepting of others and their rights to live in peace regardless of beliefs, cultures, identify or way of life.
